Disney Streaming

Disney Streaming is a collective for four major products:

  • Disney+
  • ESPN+
  • Star+
  • Hulu

Scale

  • 180,000,000 subscribers
  • 3 continents:
    • Americas
    • Western Europe
    • Asia Pacific

Structure


A service team is a team, consists of 2 to 3 engineers, a tech lead and a project manager. This is commonly described as a two pizza team.

Responsibilities


A service team is responsible for everything their API/APP needs, this includes:

  • Feature development
  • Infrastructure
  • Observability
  • Deployments
  • On call/escalations
  • ...

Cloud

Disney Streaming backend API's are hosted on AWS, and Disney are one of their largest customers.

Why the cloud?

There are many reasons why a company may choose to run their workloads on the cloud, but for me there are two huge advantages to adopting cloud computing

  • Rich set of APIs to service infrastructure needs
  • Scalability and Elasticity
